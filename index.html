    About Economics

    The Economics Department offers undergraduate degrees in the Darla Moore School of Business and the College of Arts and Sciences. In addition, the department has strong graduate programs, offering master's and doctral degrees.

    Undergraduate Program
    The undergraduate programs train students to think critically on a wide range of economic topics. Our B.A. and B.S. degrees place an emphasis on the application of economic theory to issues in business and public policy. These programs preparesstudents for professional careers in finance, consulting, government, law, and teaching.

    MA Program
    The MA program offers a curriculum that combines economic theory with strong empirical training that prepares students for careers in the public and private sector. MA students have the option of earnign a graduate certificate in business analytics as part of their studies.

    Ph.D. Program
    The Ph.D. program prepares students for academic careers. The program developes students skill sets in the areas of economic theory, statistical methods, and topical coures in fields of economics.
